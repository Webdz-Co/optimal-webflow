How It Works
Your first step at Optimal Force Fitness is receiving your Free Personalized 1-on-1 consultation.
Here is what you can expect in your intro session:
  1. Your personal training consultant will ask you a series of questions in order to assess your fitness experience (what has worked, what has not,) your commitment, and any limitations you may have.
  2. Then your new trainer will take you through a FREE workout and assessment to determine your baseline.
  3. Once you have completed your fitness assessment, we will sit down with you and establish your GOAL and layout your customized success plan based on our analysis of what a realistic expectation is for your results.
