Position:

Fitness Instructor/Trainer (Part-time)

Location:

Optimal Force Fitness
1071 Foch Street
Fort Worth, TX 76107
[Behind Cryo and South of Chimy's]

Hours:

12-20 hours per week
M-F: Between 6am-8pm;
Weekends will vary

Description:

We're looking for committed and dedicated individuals to help grow our business, so we can share the most up-to-date form of resistance training using ARX equipment to those in the Fort Worth area.

As an Instructor/Trainer at Optimal Force Fitness you will work one-on-one with members to help them achieve their fitness goals in a safe and efficient manner.

Responsibilities
/Duties:

  • Consult with clients regarding their fitness goals
  • Instruct them on how to properly and safely use the equipment
  • Document client training sessions properly
  • Assist in Front Desk related activities including:
  • - Answer phones in a friendly manner and assist callers with a variety of questions
    - Register new clients
    - Check clients into the system
    - Respond to client questions and concerns in a timely and professional manner and elevate to a Manager/Owner as needed.

Requirements:

  • Passionate about health and fitness
  • Commitment to on-going training and development
  • Excellent coaching and customer service skills
  • Punctuality and reliability is a must
  • Strong listener with the ability to problem solve
  • High School diploma/GED equivalent
  • Must be 18 years of age or older
  • CPR Certification (if hired)

Preferred:

  • Accredited Personal Training Certification (i.e. - NASM, ACE, ACSM, etc.)
  • Degree (or working towards a degree) in Kinesiology, Exercise & Sports Studies, Physical Therapy, Exercise Physiology, Physical Education

How to Apply:

  1. Fill-out the application online, or if you prefer download a PDF version of the application here. Feel free to add a short statement explaining why you wish to work with us.
  2. Submit your application in-person at our center or via E-mail to support@optimalforce.co
  3. A follow-up E-mail will be sent confirming we received your completed application.
