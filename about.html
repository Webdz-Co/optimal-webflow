Optimal Force Fitness opened in March 2017 to bring science and technology based training to Fort Worth in a safe, effective, and efficient manner. Our primary equipment is ARX (adaptive resistance exercise) which measures each exercise and allows you to track your progress. We're located in the back of the Foch Street Warehouses south of 7th Street between the Cultural District and Downtown Fort Worth.

Co-Founded by Sharon Pruit and her son Wacey Pruit. Sharon has a background in Physical Therapy and Personal Training. Wacey follows biohacking technology and health trends. Once they found the ARX equipment and technology was not in Fort Worth, they set out to change that.

Come give it a trial to see for yourself.
ARX-Fit (Adaptive Resistance Exercise) Training sessions incorporate the most effective and efficient training technology to date in order to provide you all the benefits of resistance training (increased strength & muscle function, improving metabolic efficiency in order to lose fat and gain muscle, increased joint strength, bone-density, and lean muscle mass, as well as enhancing hormonal function) in 1-2, twenty-minute sessions per week. ARX-Fit achieves this through adaptive resistance, meaning we provide you the exact amount of resistance that you can produce every second – so your body reaches the amount of exercise necessary in less time than traditional exercise. Along with time-efficient, highly effective adaptive resistance, we also track your performance and store it to give you “real-time” progress every session.
  • Whole-body Vibration for accelerated results and recovery
  • Body Composition Tracking to measure progress in body fat and BMI
  • Surge 360 hydraulic training to get leaner, faster and stronger – injury free – with total body intervals that train cardio and muscles simultaneously (Coming Soon)
